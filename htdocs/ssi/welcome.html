MIMAS 3.0 is a freely available system for annotation of GeneChip and BeadArray experiments in a MIAME-compliant manner. It provides users with a convenient one-step data export and upload procedure to the EBI's ArrayExpress repository.

The systems includes a front-end web application, as well as administration and curation tools. MIMAS 3.0 works with all standards-compatible web browsers. If you use MIMAS, please quote Hermida et al. BMC Bioinformatics 2006 (and Gattiker et al, in preparation).
