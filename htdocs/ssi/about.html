The system is currently installed at Vital-IT (SIB, Lausanne) and the the bioinformatics platform of Ouest-genopole (IRISA, Rennes).

MIMAS is currently developed within a joint effort by Jacques Rougemont, Alexandre Gattiker, Patrick Descombes, Michael Primig and Hugues Leroy.

MIMAS was developed jointly by the University of Basel and the Swiss Institute of Bioinformatics, under the supervision of Michael Primig, by Leandro Hermida and Alexandre Gattiker.

MIMAS is currently maintained by Robin Liechti.

Olivier Schaad, Christa Niederhauser and Céline Delucinge Vivier have contributed to the development of MIMAS (many thanks!).

To download the MIMAS system please contact Michael Primig, Inserm, U625, Rennes, France.
